Welcome to Chem International

Chem International supplies primary, secondary and tertiary fatty amines to locations throughout North America. These products are suitable for use as raw materials in surfacant and derivative production, as well as a variety of direct applications. We partner with international and domestic producers who utilize the latest technologies and adhere to strict quality standards.
